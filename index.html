About me

As a dedicated Mechanical Engineer, I earned my degree from the esteemed University of Maiduguri in November 2015. My professional journey commenced at Jaiz Bank plc, where I served as a Transaction Officer in the Operations department. During my time there, I encountered numerous advertisements highlighting the significance of coding and programming, which immediately captivated my interest and ignited a desire for learning.

Motivated to expand my skill set and pursue new avenues of growth, I embarked on a relentless pursuit of knowledge through various online resources and self-directed learning. With a resolute determination, I independently acquired proficiency in coding and programming, a journey that has significantly enriched my capabilities and aspirations.

This continuous journey of self-improvement and passion for learning has enabled me to harness the synergy between my Mechanical Engineering background and coding skills. As I transitioned to the role of Mechanical Engineer II with Rural Water Supply and Sanitation Agency (RUWASAA) Borno, I have consistently sought opportunities to apply my expertise in both domains, fostering a holistic approach to problem-solving and innovation.

In summary, my journey as a self-learner has been an inspiring and fulfilling experience, empowering me to confidently explore new realms of possibilities and contribute meaningfully to the fields of Mechanical Engineering and technology.
